Standard Cleaning Checklist

  • Dust all furniture, shelves, and tabletops.
  • Vacuum carpets, rugs, and mopping hard floor surfaces.
  • Scrub toilets, showers, bathtubs, and sinks.
  • Wipe down kitchen counters and appliance exteriors.
  • Empty waste baskets and replace liners.
  • Quick straighten up and make unmade beds.

Deep Cleaning Checklist

  • All Standard Cleaning tasks included.
  • Wipe baseboards, door frames, and window sills.
  • Sanitize all light switches, plugs, and door handles.
  • Deep scrub bathroom tile grout and soap scum.
  • Dust vent covers, ceiling fans, and high blinds.
  • Clean range hood exterior and detailed stovetop scrub.

Move-In / Move-Out Checklist

  • Standard & Deep Clean items included.
  • Clean inside all kitchen cabinets, drawers, and shelves.
  • Clean inside the oven, broiler, and storage drawer.
  • Clean inside the refrigerator, shelves, and crisper boxes.
  • Clean and dust empty bedroom closets and clothing rods.
  • Remove all cobwebs and wipe clean internal glass panes.
